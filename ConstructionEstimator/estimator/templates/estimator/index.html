Why Us

Working in Estimating and Sales can be very exhausting. You get thousands of bids and win less than one percent of those bids. General Contractors rely on you to help them arrange a budget for the owner and more times than not, they don't even win the bids.But you need to keep submitting those bids so you can maintain the reationship with that company! After submitting so many proposals, you could easily get tired and discouraged. That is why you need us. We are experts at estimating and following up with the GC and we don't get tired or discouraged. We will bid all your ITBz and ask the GC for more. And we will follow up on those bids and confirm with the GC if they won the project. Once we confirm the GC won, we follow up until we win.

PRICING PLAN


Construction Business Partners Pricing

Our pricing model is unique. Why pay for estimating hundrends of jobs that you do not win. We do not charge you upfront fees for estimating your jobs or following up with the GC for you. We understand that not all jobs you bid come out as an Awarded contract. So we invented a partnership pricing model. We only get paid commissions when you get awarded the contract. The commission is Negotiable.
